Groom & Bride

Vipul Saxena

It’s only fitting that my first work crush ended up being my last. I saw Garima as she walked into the workplace, and immediately but unsuccessfully attempted to make her mine. I guess it just wasn’t our time. We slaved away at work for a while and then we followed different roads - I obviously took the fun road. Some time down the line, to my great surprise, Garima resurfaced from what I imagined to be a white abode in the clouds, as I was going through some dark days back then. She became a consistent figure in my life thereafter and shone brighter than the sun. Days turned to months and months to years to a point where I now know that my to-be wife likes pasta and Netflix more than me.

Garima Dhawan

It all started with a little love and some tenderness. It was just yesterday, I saw you sitting there, your head was down, your eyes were red. No comb had touched your hair. You were on your little computer designing things or whatever it is that you do. When you picked me up in your Brio and took me for our very first date to Neemrana, how did all that happen? And now it’s been almost 4 years. I know a thing or two about you now like how my husband to be will listen to his friends for Netflix recommendations when I have been recommending the same show to him for eons? He would go, “Isn’t this show great, babe? My friend told me about it”. That’s when I wanted to gut him, but when he asked to Hold My Hand for the rest of my life, I instantly knew I wanted to love him the best I could.
